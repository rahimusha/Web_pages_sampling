Accurate Simulation: Real devices provide a more accurate simulation of user experience compared to emulators or simulators.

Hardware Compatibility:  Testing on real devices ensures compatibility with various hardware configurations, such as screen sizes, resolutions, and sensors.

Performance Testing: Real devices allow for more accurate performance testing, including CPU, memory, and battery usage.

User Experience: User Experience:Real devices provide insights into the actual user experience, including touch responsiveness, gestures, and navigation.

Regression Testing: Real devices are essential for regression testing to ensure that new updates or features do not break existing functionality.

Customer Satisfaction: By testing on real devices, you can ensure a higher quality of your application, leading to improved customer satisfaction and retention.
